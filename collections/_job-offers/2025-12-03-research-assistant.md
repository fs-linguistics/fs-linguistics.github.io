## Details
The Pragmatic inference lab (Dr. Asya Achimova) is looking for a research assistant for 20 hours/month starting December 2025. The initial contract is planned for 6 months with the possibility of extension. We are looking for an assistant with data analysis experience and strong organizational skills to take over the role of a lab manager.

Your tasks will include:
- Data processing
- Maintaining git repositories
- Streamlining data analysis, cleaning and annotating code (R, JavaScript, HTML)
- Updating the lab website
- Maintaining zotero databases
- Keeping track of project progress
- Scheduling meetings & interviews

You can learn more about our work at [https://asya-achimova.github.io/](https://asya-achimova.github.io/)

If you are interested in this position, please send an email to asya.achimova@uni-tuebingen.de.
